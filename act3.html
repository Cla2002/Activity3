Adobo

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made with meat, usually chicken or pork, marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns, then simmered until tender.

Sinigang na Baboy

Sinigang na Baboy is a sour soup native to the Philippines. It is made with pork, various vegetables, and tamarind for its distinctive sour taste.

Cake Yum

Cake Yum is a popular Filipino dessert made with layers of sponge cake, custard filling, and topped with a creamy frosting made of condensed milk and cream cheese.
